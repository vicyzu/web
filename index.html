Research

The Vision and Intelligent Computing (VIC) Lab is a research laboratory within the Department of Electrical Engineering, dedicated to advancing cutting-edge technologies in Computer Vision, Multimedia, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Intelligent Computing. Our research focuses on the integration of image, video, and audio processing with innovative deep learning techniques and intelligent systems.

Our mission is to develop intelligent, efficient, and scalable AI-driven solutions that address real-world challenges in fields such as healthcare, geoscience, human-computer interaction, and autonomous systems. We actively collaborate with academic institutions, industry partners, and research communities to push the boundaries of intelligent computing and multimedia technologies.
